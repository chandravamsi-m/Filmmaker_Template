The Filmmaker

Marcus
Holloway.

Award-winning documentary filmmaker based in New York. Specializing in human rights, culture, and investigative documentaries. Festival screenings worldwide.

With over 15 years in the field, my work focuses on the intersection of personal narrative and systemic change. I believe that the most powerful stories are those that challenge our preconceptions.
